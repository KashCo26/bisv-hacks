Healthcare Access and Marginalized Crime

Healthcare Access in the US

Proper healthcare can often be hard to find outside of home, or even in a different country. Even in the US, not all healthcare is created equal, for there are still places where it is hard to find proper and affordable care. Rural areas in the US are ranked the lowest in Healthcare Accessibility, because in these areas, there are fewer hospitals, making it harder to quickly access healthcare.

Marginalized Crime

Crime rates are going up in many places, and police employment rates are going down. Crimes happens almost every second, and they include robbery, assualt, vehicle theft, and larceny theft among many. The amount of corruption in many police departments makes it hard for some groups of people to call the police.
